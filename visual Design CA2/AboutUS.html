About Us

Welcome to Urban Brews, your comfiest neighborhood coffee shop where we always serve high-quality, handmade coffee with a side of friendly smiles. Located in Dublin City , Urban Brews is a place where all kinnd of coffee lovers meet and come together to enjoy not only a lovely brewed coffee but also a sense of community and connection to those who visit.

Our Mission

At Urban Brews, we are passionate about coffee and or coummunity. Our mission is to provide the greatest coffee experience while creating a warm, welcoming space for every customer who walks through our doors. From our naturally sourced irish beans to our signature blends, we strive to make every cup a lasting immpression to each of our customers . Whether you're here for a morning pick-ups, a quick cup before work or a relaxing break, we want Urban Brews to be your go-to destination for a great coffee and vibes.

Our Story

Founded in 2016 by 5 local friends that had great taste for coffee, Urban Brews began as a small, community-oriented café. What started as a big dream has since grown into a beloved spot in the hearth of Dublin city. We strongly believe that coffee is more than just a drink it's a way to connect with others, share stories, have laughs and build memories. Over the years, we’ve expanded our bussiness but our commitment to quality and community remains the same at the heart of everything we do.
